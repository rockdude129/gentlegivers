The Gentle Givers Foundation:

A Project by Nandan, Rohan, & Alex

NOTE: This project is a WOP (Work In Progress). To contact, please use the signupform below or click the links at the bottom of the page.

The Gentle Givers Foundation is a non-profit organization that collects clothing,
toys, and other items to give to children in need. While doing this, we collect these
items from elementary and middle schools, educating kids on why doing this is good
and how their actions can help kids in need.
