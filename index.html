The Gentle Givers



A Non-Profit by:
Nandan, Rohan, & Alex

What do we do?



At the Gentle Givers Foundation, we are highschool students who want to give back to our community that has supported, and continues to support us. We believe that one of the largest issues in our community is people not being able to afford items for their children. As a solution, we donate clothes and toys that we collect from elementary and middle schools to families with a lesser income, trying to help children be less restricted by their family's wealth.
